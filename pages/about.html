About SFR Jalama

A working ranch. A lot of open space. No shortage of character.

SFR Jalama is a hidden slice of old California tucked between the Santa Ynez hills and the Pacific coast — 170 acres of living, breathing ranch land that doesn't ask you to slow down so much as it just makes slowing down feel obvious.

We host overnight guests, weddings and gatherings, corporate retreats, and photography and film shoots. The ranch has been shaped over the years into something that works for all of it — enough space to spread out, enough character to make it feel like somewhere.

If you've been looking for a backdrop that doesn't look like every other venue, or just a place to get out of the city and wake up somewhere worth waking up — this is it.
