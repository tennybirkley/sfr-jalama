About SFR Jalama

A working ranch. A lot of open space. No shortage of character.

Just off Highway 1 on California's Central Coast, the Stoll family stumbled onto this place in 2020 and knew immediately they weren't letting it go. 170 acres of oak woodland, cattle pastures, a swimming and fishing pond, a year-round creek, and hiking trails with views that make it hard to leave.

The land sits where the rolling chaparral and oak-studded hills of the Santa Ynez meet the cool breeze coming off the Pacific. Temperate year-round. Quiet in the way that only 170 acres can be quiet.

Since opening, we've hosted weddings, company retreats, family reunions, and marketing and film shoots. Whatever you're building — we're glad to share it with you.

Cate, Matt, RJ, Maddy, Jacqui, and Cece.
